Welcome to Chewie Chewsday

Unleash your inner gum aficionado as we bring you a line-up of tantalizing contests that will have you blowing bubbles of all sizes and styles. From jaw-dropping bubble-blowing battles to artistic gum sculpture showdowns, this festival promises a gum-chomping, bubble-popping extravaganza like never before.

it's not all about the bubblegum magic join us for insightful talks and workshops, where bubblegum enthusiasts and experts share their chewy wisdom. Learn about the history of bubblegum, the science of that perfect pop, and how this beloved treat has become a cultural icon over the years.
